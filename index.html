ABOUT US

Pekich Properties is a mother/son team from Palos Verdes. We specialize in the real estate markets of Los Angeles, with exceptional knowledge of the South Bay.

Our areas of expertise include: Palos Verdes Estates, Rolling Hills, Rancho Palos Verdes, San Pedro, Beach Cities, Torrance, Lomita, and Harbor City.

We aim to treat each client as though they are family, listening to needs and wants to create a satisfying real estate experience.

Whether buying or selling we want our clients experience to be exciting and stress free.
